KBBQs

1. Quality AYCE: Gangnam Station KBBQ

This is the best quality All you can eat Korean BBQ! The wait is very long though but you can put your name on the yelp wait list in advance. I did wait 2 hours one time. The banchan is also loaded. It's AYCE and you can grab any banchan whenever you want.

2. Cheap AYCE: Wi Korean BBQ & Meat Love

This is the best cheap AYCE Korean BBQ! As a college student I love eating AYCE with my friends but it's usually so expensive. This is the best affordable and budget friendly kbbq spot. Per person it's $25.

Comfort Food

1. Hangari Kalguksu

This is my favorite restaurant in Ktown LA! The soup is so good and the bossam is delicious. The food always hits.

2. Surawon

This spot is so much better than BCD Tofu House. The soup is more authentic and the meat tastes so much better. The meat is super juicy.
